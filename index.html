PCC Renewal

Christians in The Presbyterian Church in Canada (PCC) committed to reforming and renewing the Church according to God's Word

Our Mission

Retrieve the riches of the Reformed tradition

Revive PCC churches by encouraging active attendance and evangelism

Reform the PCC by pointing to Scripture and advocating for orthodoxy

Our Beliefs

We believe the Bible is the word of God, and our sole authoritative rule in faith and life

We hold to the 3 Ecumenial Creeds, the 5 SOLAs, and the Reformed confessions

We accept differing views in tertiary issues such as evolution, certain eschatology, etc.

The Church's primary duties are worship and evangelism; social and ecological justice must be rooted in our calling as faithful stewards of the gospel

We hold to the biblical definition of marriage, biblical sexual ethics, and view abortion as a violation of the sixth commandment
